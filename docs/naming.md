# Product Naming Convention

## Core

1. **Tracehound** (The Core)
   Role: Security Buffer & Quarantine.
   Metaphor: The hunting dog. It picks up the scent, catches the threat, and never lets go.

## Satellites (Role-Based)

2. **Argos** (The Observer)
   Role: Runtime Behavioral Observer.
   Metaphor: Panoptes (the all-seeing). Watches the Event Loop and Worker Threads without ever blinking.

3. **Talos** (The Enforcer)
   Role: External Policy-Driven Response.
   Metaphor: The bronze automaton of Crete. Emotionless and decision-free; it executes given commands (Block/Throttle) with mechanical precision.

4. **Huginn** (The Scout)
   Role: External Threat Feed Integration.
   Metaphor: Odin's "Thought" raven. Roams the world, gathers external threat intelligence (AbuseIPDB, etc.) and whispers it back to the system.

5. **Muninn** (The Archivist)
   Role: Threat Metadata Substrate & Archive.
   Metaphor: Odin's "Memory" raven. Never forgets the past; preserves attack patterns, timelines, and statistics.

6. **Heimdall** (The Guardian)
   Role: Supply Chain Security.
   Metaphor: The watchman of the Bifrost. Guards the gates of the dependency supply chain — CI/CD monitoring, package scanning, vulnerability reporting.

7. **Loki** (The Trickster)
   Role: Passive Deception & Tarpit Layer.
   Metaphor: The shape-shifter. Plants invisible canaries and sticky tarpits to unmask attackers and waste their resources — all at near-zero cost.

## Control-Based

8. **Norns** (The Weavers)
   Role: Readiness Synthesis.
   Metaphor: The three fate-weavers of Norse mythology. They assess past, present, and future to determine system readiness.

9. **Furies** (The Punishers)
   Role: Adversarial Stress Testing.
   Metaphor: The Erinyes of Greek mythology. They relentlessly test system defenses through adversarial scenarios.
